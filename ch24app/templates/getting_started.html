Getting Started

Here's a step-by-step for using this website:

1. Click ‘Register’ to create a login and password.

2. Click ‘Add Channel’ to enter your channel name and contact information.

What is a Channel?

A Channel is a dedicated space where you can explore a collection of related Programs and Episodes. Think of Channels as specialized hubs focused on specific topics or interests, similar to YouTube Channels where you can follow your favorite creators and their content. You may be the producer of multiple ‘Channels’, with different target audiences.

3. Click ‘Add Program’ to enter the name of your first program. A program can have one or more episodes.

4. Click ‘Add Episode’ to enter the name of your first program episode.

5. Click ‘Upload Media’ to upload media containing your episode.

6. If the ‘View Media Info’ button is RED, click ‘View Media Info’, review media information for your uploaded media and adjust your export settings.

7. If the ‘View Episode’ button is present, click ‘View Episode’ to preview your episode.

By following these instructions, you will submit your first program and episode to Atlanta 24 Community Media.
