Getting Started with Atlanta 24 Community Media

Here's a step-by-step for using this website:

1. Click ‘Register’ to create a login and password.
2. Click ‘Add Channel’ to enter your channel name and contact information.
3. Click ‘Add Program’ to enter the name of your first program.
4. Click ‘Add Episode’ to enter the name of your first program episode.
5. Click ‘My Episodes’ to view your episodes.
6. Click ‘Upload Media’ to upload media containing your episode.
7. Click ‘View Media Info’ to review media information for your uploaded media.

By following these instructions, you will submit your first program and episode to Atlanta 24 Community Media.
